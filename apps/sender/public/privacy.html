Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 20, 2026 · Effective: July 20, 2026

Ctrl+ArcZ is a non-custodial app for protected USDC transfers on the Arc network. We built it to move money safely, not to profile you. We collect no personal identity, hold no keys, and keep the data we do touch to the minimum the service needs.

✓  Non-custodial · no accounts · no personal identity collected

01Who we are & what this covers

This policy explains how the Ctrl+ArcZ mobile app and website (together, the “Service”) handle information. Ctrl+ArcZ is a self-custodial tool: you connect your own wallet (such as MetaMask or Rabby) and it, not us, holds your keys and authorizes every transaction. The Service currently operates on the Arc test network, using test assets that hold no monetary value.

02Information we do not collect

  • No personal identity — no name, email, phone number, date of birth, or government ID.
  • No private keys or seed phrases — your keys never leave your wallet; we cannot see or access them.
  • No precise location, contacts, photos library, or advertising identifiers.
  • No trackers or third-party analytics/advertising SDKs, and we never sell data.

03Information we process

To provide the Service, we process a small amount of technical data:

Data Why Where it lives
Public wallet address To run the address-poisoning firewall against public on-chain history, prepare transfers, and show your activity. In your browser/device and, transiently, on our backend during a request.
Transaction details (amounts, recipient addresses) To create, escrow, claim, and settle protected transfers you initiate. Recorded on the public blockchain (permanent and public by design).
Push notification token (only if you enable notifications) To alert you when a payment involves your address. Stored on our backend, linked to your address, until you turn it off or uninstall.
Camera (only when you scan a QR code) To read a recipient address so the firewall can check it before you send. Processed on-device in real time; images are not stored or transmitted.
Basic technical logs (e.g. IP address for rate limiting) Security and abuse prevention on our backend. Kept briefly on our server, then discarded.

04How we use it

We use the data above solely to operate and secure the Service: checking recipients with the firewall, relaying and settling transfers you request, and — if you opt in — delivering notifications. We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or resale, and we do not build a personal profile of you.

05Blockchain is public and permanent

Transactions you make are written to a public blockchain. That data — your wallet address, amounts, and transfer history — is visible to anyone, permanent, and outside our control. We cannot edit or delete on-chain records. This is inherent to how blockchains work, not a choice we make.

06Third-party services

The Service relies on a few infrastructure providers. When you use the relevant feature, limited technical data (such as your wallet address or a signed request) is shared with them to make it work. Each has its own privacy policy:

  • WalletConnect / Reown — relays the connection between the app and your wallet. (policy)
  • Arc RPC & ArcScan explorer — read and broadcast public on-chain data.
  • Circle — powers cross-chain (CCTP) transfers and gasless claims. (policy)
  • Expo — delivers push notifications, if you enable them. (policy)

07Security

  • Your keys stay in your own wallet; we can never move your funds.
  • All traffic to our backend and the network uses encrypted TLS; the mobile app additionally pins the connection to defend against interception.
  • You are responsible for safeguarding your wallet, its recovery phrase, and your device. No app can recover a lost key.

08Data retention & your choices

  • Disconnect any time — disconnecting your wallet removes it from the current session.
  • Notifications — turn them off in the app, or uninstall, to stop and remove your push token from our backend. You may also request deletion via the contact below.
  • On-chain data cannot be deleted by us or anyone; it is permanent by design.

09Children

The Service is not directed to children and is not intended for anyone under 18. We do not knowingly process data from minors.

10Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as the Service evolves. Material changes will be reflected by the “Last updated” date at the top of this page. Continued use after an update means you accept the revised policy.
